## Wisdom (Communities)

- [r/DestructiveReaders](https://reddit.com/r/DestructiveReaders)
  Rigorous critique subreddit: you must critique 1:1 before posting your own work, and critiques are expected to be honest and high-effort. Best-in-class for line-level feedback on English-language excerpts.
